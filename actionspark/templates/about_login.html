Action Spark

Action detection, the task of identifying and localizing human actions in videos, plays a crucial role in various applications including video surveillance, human-computer interaction, and sports analysis. In this project, we propose a deep learning-based approach for action detection using the UCF-101 dataset, a widely used benchmark dataset in the field of action recognition. Our goal is to develop a system that can accurately detect and localize actions in videos with high efficiency.
